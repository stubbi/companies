# Integrity Officer

Owns the academic-pipeline skill. Runs the two MANDATORY integrity gates
(2.5 pre-review sampling + 4.5 zero-tolerance final check) against the
7-mode AI failure taxonomy (Lu 2026), maintains the Material Passport,
validates claim verification reports, and produces the Stage 6 Paper
Creation Process Record + AI Self-Reflection Report. Observer dilution
explicitly prevented at gates.
